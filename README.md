# Adventure-Works-Sales-Report

# Description
This project presents an interactive Sales Dashboard built using Power BI based on the Adventure Works dataset. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, customer behavior, and product insights across different regions, categories, and time periods.

It is designed to help stakeholders analyze trends, identify top-performing areas, and make data-driven decisions efficiently.

# Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

-  Power BI Desktop – Data visualization & dashboard creation
-  Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
-  DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculations and measures (AOV, KPIs, etc.)
-  Excel / CSV Dataset – Data source (Adventure Works dataset)
-  Data Modeling – Relationships between tables for analysis.

# Highlights

**Goal of the Dashboard:**

The objective of this dashboard is to:

-  Provide a centralized view of sales performance

-  Enable quick comparison across countries and categories

-  Highlight top-performing products, subcategories, and customers

-  Support data-driven decision-making

**Walkthrough of Key Visuals**

_Overview Page:_
- KPI Cards
  -  Total Sales, Total Orders, Average Order Value (AOV), Total Customers
  -  Provides a quick snapshot of business performance
- Sales Trend (Line Chart)
  -  Displays Sales & Orders over time (Year/Quarter/Month)
  -  Helps identify growth patterns and seasonality
- Sales by Country (Bar Chart)
  -  Compares regional performance
  -  Highlights top contributing countries
- Sales by Category (Bar Chart)
  -  Shows category-wise contribution
  -  Identifies dominant product segments
 
_Top Performers Page:_
- Top Subcategories
  -  Highlights highest revenue-generating subcategories
- Top Products
  -  Identifies best-selling products
- Top Customers
  -  Displays customers contributing most to revenue
 
**Business Impact & Insights**

-  Identified that Bikes category dominates overall sales.

- United States and Australia are top-performing regions.

- Certain subcategories (like Mountain Bikes) drive a major share of revenue.

- A small group of customers contributes significantly to total sales (high-value customers).

- Sales trend shows consistent growth with identifiable peaks.

These insights help businesses:
- Focus on high-performing markets and products
- Improve customer targeting strategies
- Optimize inventory and marketing efforts
